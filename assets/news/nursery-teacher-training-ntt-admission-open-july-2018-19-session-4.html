Nursery Teacher Training NTT Admission Open July 2018-19 Session

NTT ( Nursery Teacher Training ) Admission Open

Rural Urban Council of Skills & Vocational Studies Institute invites the application for NTT Nursery Teacher Training Courses Admission in India. Its a 1 year or 2 years duration course.

NTT Course syllabus help the teachers to understand the child's growth and developmental needs like physical development, motor development, social growth, emotional needs etc.

NTT course also help to understand child behavior and their adjustment patterns. 


Eligibility Criteria for NTT Course Admission:
Min. 12th Pass or higher education.
No age bar for apply.

Duration NTT Course :
Its One Year or Two Year Duration Course.

Document Required for NTT Course :
10th Class Certificate & Marksheet.
12th Class Certificate & Marksheet.
5 Passport Size Photographs.
Adhaar Card Copy.
Filled Admission Form.

Contact for NTT Admission: 09560192015
